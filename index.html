Mysteryle
ˈmɪstəriʊl
Mys•ter•y•le
MISS-ter-ee-ull

This is a game based on Wordle, but each day has its own twist rather than its own answer.
You can play the game more than once on the same day, and you are almost gauranteed to get a different answer, but the twist is the same.
Can you figure out each day's twist? Some of them may be based on other Wordle-inspired games. Five greens and winning do not necessarily imply each other; the game will tell you if you win. The hint button will present a normal Wordle where the answer is the five-letter hint for the twist.
You can play any day's twist, or you can specify the associated word (i.e. the hint) to play that twist.
